Privacy Policy

WAIV is built to be personal without being invasive. We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal user data. The only thing WAIV requires is access to your Apple Music account so the app can create and play your DJ-style listening experience.

1. What WAIV does

WAIV lets you connect your Apple Music account so the app can create and play personalized DJ-style listening experiences using your music library and listening context.

2. Information we collect

WAIV does not collect personal information such as:

  • name
  • email address
  • phone number
  • location
  • uploaded content
  • in-app profiles or account details

WAIV also does not sell, rent, or share user data with advertisers or data brokers.

3. Apple Music connection

To use WAIV, you must connect your Apple Music account. This connection is required so WAIV can access the music needed to power the experience.

WAIV uses Apple Music access only to enable app functionality, such as:

  • reading your available music library and catalog context
  • generating stations or DJ experiences based on your music
  • playing music through Apple Music

WAIV does not independently store your Apple Music account credentials.

4. In-app purchases and subscriptions

WAIV may offer in-app purchases or subscriptions. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

WAIV does not collect, process, or store your payment card information or billing details. Payment information is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple’s own terms and privacy practices.

5. Analytics and tracking

WAIV does not use third-party advertising trackers or create personal behavioral advertising profiles.

If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated before those tools are introduced.

6. Data storage

Because WAIV does not collect personal user data, we do not maintain personal data records for app users beyond what is technically necessary for the app to function in real time.

7. Children’s privacy

WAIV is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
