Support

Need help with WAIV? We can help with playback issues, Apple Music connection problems, subscriptions, and bug reports.

Get help

The fastest way to reach us is through the contact form. We read every message and typically respond within 24–48 hours.

Include as much detail as you can, especially what device you’re using and what went wrong.

Common help topics

  • Apple Music connection or authorization issues
  • Playback interruptions or stations not loading
  • Subscription and billing questions handled through Apple
  • Bug reports, crashes, or unexpected behavior

Subscriptions and billing

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. If you need to manage billing, renewals, or cancellations, use your App Store subscription settings.

What WAIV needs to work

WAIV requires access to your Apple Music account in order to generate and play your radio-style experience. If playback or setup is failing, make sure your Apple Music access is active and available on your device.
