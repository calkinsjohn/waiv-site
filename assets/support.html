Support

Need help with WAIV? We can help with playback issues, Apple Music connection problems, subscriptions, and bug reports.

Quick help

1. How does WAIV work?

WAIV turns your music library into a live radio experience. AI DJs build real, flowing sessions from your saved songs so everything feels connected, not random.

2. Do I need Apple Music?

Yes. WAIV uses your Apple Music library to create your station. You’ll need an active Apple Music subscription.

3. Why isn’t my music showing up?

Make sure:

  • You’re signed into Apple Music
  • Your library is synced
  • WAIV has permission to access your music

If it’s still not working, try restarting the app.

4. Why isn’t playback working?

Check:

  • Your Apple Music subscription is active
  • You have an internet connection
  • Playback works in the Apple Music app

If everything looks right and it still won’t play, send us a message.

5. Can I choose different DJs?

Yes. Each DJ has a different vibe. You can switch anytime to change the feel of your station.

6. Can I control what plays next?

You can skip tracks or switch DJs at any time, or just let WAIV keep the flow going.
